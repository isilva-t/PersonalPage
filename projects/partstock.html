Auto Parts Manager

Sep 2025 - Present

Inventory management system that expanded operational capacity for automotive parts SME, driving 50% sales growth in first month. Team of 5 employees manages diverse inventory - from budget items to high-value components (engines, transmission kits) - processing 200+ monthly transactions.

Business Impact & Features

  • • Operational Efficiency: Responsive interface for warehouse cataloging and real-time search, eliminating unnecessary floor-to-warehouse trips
  • • OLX Integration: Automated bulk publishing + market metrics (views, saved listings, contact clicks) for pricing decisions
  • • Growing Catalog: 2,000+ parts currently, projected 10,000 by 2026 with hierarchical organization
  • • Image Pipeline: Compression for performance + originals for documentation
  • • Smart Alerts: Intelligent warnings for incomplete data during cataloging
  • • Production Ready: Daily automated backups, deployed on AWS infrastructure

Key Achievement: Built complete full-stack solution from requirements gathering to production deployment. System handles real business operations daily, demonstrating ability to deliver end-to-end applications with measurable business impact.
